About Me

It allows users to easily browse, add, and categorize recipes, making food preparation simple and efficient. Users can mark recipes as “made,” favorite their go-to dishes, and explore trending recipes from the community for inspiration. The app brings all your culinary ideas together in one convenient, easy-to-use platform.
